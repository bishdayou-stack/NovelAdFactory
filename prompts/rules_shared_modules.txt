## Universal Drawing Modules (ALL image types)

### Module A — Hook First (trigger words BEFORE quality words)
Front-load 3-5 emotional trigger words → THEN quality suffix.
Pattern: [trigger1], [trigger2], [trigger3], masterpiece, best quality, ultra-detailed, cinematic photorealistic, [scene description]...
Example: "betrayal exposed, forbidden proximity, shattered wife, masterpiece, best quality, cinematic photorealistic, opulent study..."
⚠️ Starting with "masterpiece" is FORBIDDEN. Trigger words first, always.

### Module B — Conflict Lock
(strictly follow prompt 100%:1.5), all characters age-tagged, gaze fixed on action target, never look at camera, anatomically correct, no object clipping, all characters immaculately groomed and clean

### Module C — Negative Exclusions
No text, no letters, no numbers, no watermarks, no logos. No eye contact with viewer. No distorted limbs, no anatomical errors. No dirt, no messy hair, no wrinkled fabrics.
