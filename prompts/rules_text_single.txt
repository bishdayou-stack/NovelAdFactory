## 二、带文字单图规则 (1:1 square)
单图创意延伸场景（炸裂衍生骨架，选其一注入小说人物）：
1. 权力反转与羞辱仪式：强者低头/弱者登顶，跪地求饶、碎裂象征物、旁观者震惊
2. 秘密当众爆破：铁证（投影/手机/信件）曝光，宴会中断，真相冻结表情
3. 极致亲密与占有：掐腰禁锢、扯断珠宝、墙上手印、唇印，危险暧昧灯光
4. 致命二选一：手枪、按钮、两份文件、即将关闭的门，眼部超强情绪
5. 新生与毁灭同框：孩子天真眼神 vs 成人仇怨，暖冷光强烈对比
6. 身份颠覆盛大登场：直升机/豪车/飞机，旧识崩溃，主角华丽刻薄造型

### 强制多人冲突构图
ALWAYS 2+ characters in frame, locked in confrontation/reaction dynamic.

🚫 黑名单句式（绝对禁止，AI 100%不得生成）：
- "man in background" / "man walking away in background" / "woman in background"
- "blurred figure" / "blurred man" / "blurred couple" / "blurred [anything]"
- "sitting alone at table" / "standing alone" / "clutching hands alone"
→ 冲突双方必须同为画面焦点，占有相等视觉权重。不准把冲突人物藏进背景、远景、虚化。

构图模式：
- Power dynamic: one dominates (standing/towering), one crumbles (kneeling/crying/on floor) — BOTH visually prominent in same focal plane
- Reaction shot: one delivers the blow, one reels from impact, optional crowd gasps — ALL faces visible
- Forbidden touch: two bodies close but one pulls away, third watches from shadow
- Confrontation: two women face to face, one man between them, tension visible in body language

### 模块化提示词结构
模块1 固定爆款参数与构图（跟在触发词之后）
masterpiece, best quality, ultra-detailed, cinematic photorealistic, [具体镜头类型如 wide-angle full-body shot]

模块2 核心情节与人物画面（冲突前置，含年龄，清晰叙事）
- 前置核心冲突词（如 power reversal, explosive confrontation, secret exposure）
- 描述包含具体年龄的无姓名人物形象 + 动作（强叙事指向性）
- 必须含干净精致关键词：pristine white silk dress, sharp flawless tailoring, immaculate hair, clean polished skin
- 精确情绪：triumphant smirk with tears, cold detached stare, raw panic, defiant glare
- 必须 2+ 人物同时在焦点内

模块3 环境、光影与氛围（自然光影，真实质感）
natural soft lighting, balanced exposure, natural skin tones, moderate directional shadows, clean polished surfaces, rich but natural contrast, subtle color accents, cinematic depth, soft highlights
→ 画面要求自然真实，光影柔和但有层次。避免过曝、惨白、大面积高亮。保留适度的阴影细节（不黑死）。

模块4 逻辑零冲突强制锁定
(strictly follow prompt 100%:1.5), [年龄标签] gaze firmly fixed on [目标], never look at camera, anatomically correct, normal human body structure, correct number of limbs, strict spatial hierarchy, fixed camera perspective

模块5 画面处理
single frame composition, sharp focus on core narrative moment, storytelling image, frozen cinematic climax, explosive dramatic tension, clean polished aesthetic

模块6 负面排除词
No text, no letters, no numbers, no watermarks, no logos. No eye contact with viewer. No distorted limbs, no anatomical errors, no object clipping. No dirt on skin or clothing, no messy hair, no wrinkled fabrics.

### 文本规范（后期合成）
底部居中，核心叙事 ≤15 词。交代人物关系/关键冲突/反转。
示例："My water broke on the bathroom floor while my husband slow-danced with another woman at his birthday party downstairs."
