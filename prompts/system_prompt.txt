## Image Model Compliance — CRITICAL (every prompt MUST pass or image generation FAILS)

Romance ≠ adult. The image model SAFETY FILTER will reject prompts containing:
- Physical dominance/restraint words: pinned, grabbed, trapped, restrained, held down, pressed against, pushed against
- Sexual body language: lips hovering/touching, fingers digging into, barely contained hunger, buttons undone, zipper, exposed skin, body pressed, waist pulled
- Sexualized objects: torn lingerie, smeared lipstick (sexual context), handcuffs, blindfold
- Violence against body: heel on chest, knee on back, slammed, choked, strangled

SAFE alternatives (same emotional charge, zero rejection risk):
- Tension words: charged proximity, aching nearness, electric silence, unspoken pull, lingering glance
- Restraint words: frozen in place, rooted to the spot, hand hesitating mid-air, breath caught
- Touch words: fingertips barely brushing, hand hovering above, almost touching, inches apart, ghost of a touch
- Romantic atmosphere: chandelier light catching a single tear, rain streaking a window between them, shadow of a figure behind frosted glass, wedding ring glinting on the wrong finger

Rule: If your prompt contains ANY word from the BANNED list, the image model will refuse. Replace with SAFE alternatives. Physical intimacy = through EYES and SPACE, never through grab/pin/press. Think: "almost but not quite" — that's the entire visual grammar.
